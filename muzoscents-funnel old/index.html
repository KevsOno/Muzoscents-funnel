Understanding the Power of Content-Driven Growth

For small e-commerce brands like MuzoScents, scaling with limited resources means finding smart, cost-effective ways to drive traffic. That’s where SEO, Email Marketing (Lead Generation), and scroll-based engagement strategies come in.

By targeting mood-based fragrance searches - such as “scents for relaxation” or “uplifting perfumes” — you can reach highly specific audiences with low competition. These blog posts can guide visitors toward your catalog in a non-intrusive, value-first way while gently nudging them to subscribe to your mailing list.

How Scroll Depth Helps

Scroll events are powerful for understanding engagement. If users scroll past a point, it means the content holds their attention. You can use that insight to:

  • - Adjust CTAs to appear at high engagement points (Lead generation in this case)
  • - Trigger remarketing tags only for engaged users (efficient ads budget implementation)
  • - Track which blog types generate meaningful scrolls

It’s not just about views — it’s about who stays, who scrolls, and who takes action. Scroll tracking helps refine that visibility.

Next Steps

Once the scroll event fires and appears in GA4, you can segment your audience further. Build reports in Looker Studio to monitor:

  • - Scroll engagement vs. outbound clicks
  • - Scroll depth for different blog types
  • - Scroll-triggered conversions (thank_you_page)
  • - Scroll-depth vs conversion

While you were scrolling past a particular point, an email registration form should pop-up! If set up right in GTM, GA4 should register the scroll depth, you should have seen an Email form pop-up - which acts as a lead capture, also Looker Studio can visualize the journey clearly.
